LIKELY is a marketing analysis application designed for people who want to gain deeper insights into their online presence. With LIKELY, users can:

    1. Analyze the performance of their recent posts.
    2. Evaluate competitor statistics.
    3. Explore engagement trends and audience sentiment.
    4. Perform Brand Tone Analysis — understand how a brand positions itself through the language used in captions, hashtags, and post content.

The app features a clean, user-friendly interface and simple navigation, making it easy to explore all its capabilities.

Pages:

    1. Login — Sign in to your account.
    2. Registration — Create a new user account.
    3. Dashboard — Overview of your activity, insights, and quick access to features.
    4. Statistics — Visual charts and reports of your social media performance.
    5. Competition Comparison — Compare your metrics with competitors or peers.
    6. Brand Tone — Analyze the tone and positioning of a brand based on the text in posts, highlighting positive, neutral, and negative content trends.
